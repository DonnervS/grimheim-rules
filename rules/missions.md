# Missions

## Encounter

### Game Area

![](media/image/encounter.png)

Play on a 30″ x 30″ battlefield.

#### Setup

Perform a Roll-off. The winner chooses to either place terrain and objectives (becoming the Defender) or select a deployment zone (becoming the Attacker). The other player takes the remaining role.

#### Terrain

There are no specific terrain restrictions in this mission.

#### Objectives

- Place D3 + 2 Objective Markers on the battlefield.
- Each marker must be at least 8″ apart and not within any Deployment Zone.

#### Looting Objectives

- Objectives in this mission can be looted once per game.

#### Scoring

Victory Points (VP) are scored at the end of each turn:

| Action | Victory Points |
|----|----|
| Control an Objective (model within 3″ and line of sight) | 1 VP per Objective |
| More models than the opponent at an Objective | \+1 VP per Objective |
| Defeat an enemy model during this turn | 2 VP |
| Complete a Side Quest | variable |

A model controls an Objective if it has at least one friendly model within 3″ and in Line of Sight to the marker.

#### Victory Condition

At the end of the game, the player with the highest total Victory Points wins.

If scores are tied, the match is a draw, or players may use defeated enemy models as a tiebreaker.

#### Special Rules

None.

## Side Quests

Players may undertake optional Side Quests during missions for additional Victory Points. These secondary objectives add tactical depth and narrative flavor to each battle.

Before deploying models for a mission, each player may select a Side Quest. Roll D33 twice and pick one entry from the table below.

Successfully completing your Side Quest during the mission awards bonus Victory Points as listed.

| D33 | Quest | Description | VP |
|----|----|----|----|
| 11 | Assassinate | Defeat one specific enemy model (chosen by opponent before deployment). | 2 |
| 12 | Lost Relic | Search for a lost item. When using INTERACT on objectives, roll D6: on 6, find the relic. Otherwise, roll on Equipment Table | 2 |
| 13 | First Blood | Be the first player to defeat an enemy model. | 2 |
| 21 | Slayer | One of your models defeats 3 or more enemy models. | 4 |
| 22 | Capture the Flag | Place a token in the enemy deployment zone. Use INTERACT to pick up. If carrier is defeated, token drops at that location. | 4 |
| 23 | Defy the Gods | Do not use any Fate Dice in a single turn. | 4 |
| 31 | Breakthrough | End the game with at least 50% of your models in the enemy deployment zone. | 6 |
| 32 | Survivors | Win without any friendly models being defeated. | 6 |
| 33 | Defender | No enemy models enter your deployment zone throughout the entire game. | 6 |
