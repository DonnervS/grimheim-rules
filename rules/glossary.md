# Glossary

Action Points (AP): Number of actions a model can perform during its activation.

Arcane Energy (AE): Energy generated by rolling 2D6 when performing CAST A SPELL to power spells.

Attacker: The model declaring a FIGHT or SHOOT action.

Block Die/Dice: Additional dice in Close Combat that can be spent to cancel opponent's successes.

Body Type: Character Builder category determining physical characteristics of a model.

Character Builder: System for creating custom models with point costs.

Class: Category of Traits defining special abilities or roles.

Concurrent Rounds: Game mechanic for simultaneous rounds with minimal downtime.

Critical Injury: Injury from critical hit, rolled on Critical Injuries Table.

Critical Success: Roll of 6, often triggers additional effects.

Defeated: Model at 0 or fewer Wounds, removed from game.

Defender: Target of an attack action.

Defense Dice (DEF): Number of D6 rolled to defend against Ranged Attacks.

Deployment Zone: Area where players set up models at game start.

Divine Energy (DE): Energy generated by rolling 2D6 when performing INVOCATION.

Equipment Table: D66 table for determining loot when plundering.

Faction: Army a model belongs to (Imperial, Undead, Primordial, Tribes, Ratlings, Mercenaries).

Fatal Damage/Wounds: Damage that cannot be blocked and ignores Defense.

Fate Die/Dice: Special dice pool rolled at round start, spent during Action Phase.

Fate Modifier: Faction-specific rules for Fate Dice with point costs.

Fate Phase: Phase at round start when Fate Dice are rolled.

Gold: Campaign currency, equivalent to point values (20 Gold = 1 VP).

Grimheim Hub: Camp between campaign missions with various locations.

Hidden: Status after HIDE action in Cover, cannot be targeted at range.

Hit Value (HTV): Target number on D6 for successful attacks.

Iconic Warband: Pre-built warband from faction chapters.

Initiative Phase: Phase at round start, dice roll determines player order.

Line of Sight (LoS): Sight line between models for attacks and effects.

Lore (Arcane/Divine): Collection of spells or divine powers for a faction.

Magic Scroll: One-use spell without energy roll risk.

Melee Range: Close combat range of 1" between bases.

Mission: Scenario with specific objectives and victory conditions.

Model: Individual miniature in game with its own stats.

Movement (MOV): Movement distance in inches per action.

Objective Marker: Terrain marker for Victory Points.

Overwatch: State after OVERWATCH action, enables Reactions.

Permanent Wound: Lasting injury from campaign aftermath.

Points (pt): Cost for models and equipment in warband building.

Range (RNG): Maximum distance of ranged weapon in inches.

Reaction: Interruption action by model in Overwatch.

Ready Model: Model that hasn't spent all AP or chosen PASS.

Roll-off: Comparison roll with 1D6 each, higher result wins.

Save (SAV): Target number on Defense dice.

Side Quest: Optional secondary objectives for additional Victory Points.

Status Effect: Persistent condition effect on a model.

Strike: Spending successful Attack Die for damage.

Tier: Category of Traits representing leadership level or experience.

Trait: Special ability of a model (maximum 5 per model).

Victory Points (VP): Points for determining winner.

Warband: Group of models belonging to one player.

Weapon Profile: Weapon statistics (RNG, ATK, HTV, DMG, CRT).

Wounds (WND): Health points of a model.
