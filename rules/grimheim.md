# Grimheim

### Skirmisher miniature game

Grimheim is a fast-paced, miniature-agnostic skirmish game set in a classic grimdark fantasy world. It features modern rules that keep both players engaged with minimal downtime.

Here are the highlights of what Grimheim offers:

- Handful of Miniatures: You command a small warband, making every model’s actions crucial.
- Miniature Agnostic: Use your favorite miniatures without restrictions.
- Fast-Paced & Dynamic: Enjoy concurrent rounds with minimal downtime, ensuring the players stay engaged.
- Easy Setup & Minimal Equipment: All you need are dice, a ruler, and your models—no special materials or tokens required.
- Customizable Warbands: Build your warband with nearly no restrictions, allowing full creative freedom.
- Heroic Battles: Models are resilient, leading to intense and strategic combats.
- Free & Open-Source: The rules are freely available and community-driven. Optional tools (printable cards, apps) can assist gameplay.
- Print & Play: Access a wide range of digital files for printing, including character models, missions, faction rules, and campaigns, as well as the basic rules.

Grimheim’s rules are open-source and welcome community contributions. For more information, visit the official Grimheim Companion at grimheim.app.
